18 Year Boy


A well-balanced vegetarian diet rich in whole plant-based foods can promote overall health and well-being for a 18-year-old boy. Here's a sample meal plan focusing on Indian cuisine and providing essential nutrients:

Remember: This is a sample, adjust portion sizes based on activity level and consult a doctor or registered dietitian for personalized advice.

Focus on Variety: Include a range of colors in your vegetables and fruits for a diverse nutrient profile.

Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day (around 3 liters).

Key: (F) - Fiber, (P) - Protein, (C) - Carbohydrates, (H) - Healthy Fats, (Ca) - Calcium, (B12) - Vitamin B12

Morning (7 AM):

Oatmeal (100 gms cooked) with: Chopped fruits (100 gms) like mango, banana, or berries (F, C, Vitamins)

Nuts and seeds (20 gms) like almonds, walnuts, and chia seeds (P, H, B12)

Milk (200 ml) or fortified plant-based milk (like soy milk) (Ca, B12)

Mid-Morning Snack (10 AM):

Sprouted moong dal (50 gms) with chopped vegetables (50 gms) like cucumber, tomato, and onion (P, F, Vitamins)

     OR

Paneer sandwich (2 slices whole wheat bread, 50 gms paneer) with a light spread of chutney (F, P, C)

Lunch (1 PM):

Dal Makhani (150 gms) with kidney beans and lentils (P, F)

Brown rice (150 gms cooked) (C, F)

Raita made with curd (100 gms) and chopped vegetables (like cucumber, carrots) (Ca, F, Vitamins)

Side salad with leafy greens (spinach) and a teaspoon of olive oil (F, Vitamins)

Evening Snack (4 PM):

Fruit Chaat (150 gms) with a variety of seasonal fruits like papaya, pomegranate, and apples (F, Vitamins)

A handful of roasted makhana (foxnuts) (20 gms) (P, H)

Dinner (7 PM):

Vegetable Pulao (150 gms cooked) with mixed vegetables and brown rice (C, F, Vitamins)

Palak Paneer (150 gms) with spinach and cottage cheese (P, Ca, Iron)

Ghee (1 tsp) for flavor (H)

Before Bed (optional):

Warm milk (200 ml) with turmeric (a pinch) (Ca, Antioxidants)

Alternative Vitamin B12 sources:

Fortified cereals

Nutritional yeast

Some types of fortified plant-based milk

Disclaimer: This plan provides an example and may not meet all your individual needs. It's recommended to consult a doctor or registered dietitian for a personalized plan considering factors like allergies, activity level, and specific health goals.
